Who We Are

Built to own and scale
great brands.

OS Enterprise Group LLC is a Georgia-based holding company established to build, own, and operate premium consumer brands. Our flagship brand — OLIDIX — represents our commitment to surgical-grade quality, honest craftsmanship, and long-term brand equity.

We believe a great brand is built on three things: the truth of its materials, the integrity of its founders, and the excellence of its execution.
